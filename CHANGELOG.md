# DriveLab Telem Changelog

## 2.3.0

- Rebuilt the Achievement Vault around 1,001 distinct driving challenges instead of repetitive speed tiers.
- Added secret, legendary, insane, multi-condition, endurance, control, impact, off-road, drift, launch, braking, and long-term progression goals.
- Preserved original tier-based achievement unlocks as a Legacy Vault record while retaining XP, levels, statistics, records, courses, and sessions.
- Changed the Achievement Vault into one continuous scrolling page so the complete challenge list remains usable on phones.
- Retuned Auto Co-Driver generation and playback so spoken calls remain active throughout a TrackLab run.
- Loosened corner and braking detection while retaining route-noise suppression and adaptive Early, Normal, and Late timing.
- Added automatic Jump, Big Jump, and Quick Dip pace notes derived from the saved reconnaissance route.
- Added numbered Auto Co-Driver callout markers to the TrackLab map with a matching map legend and note-review numbering.
- Contained the live TrackLab car-position marker inside the course map when a reset, teleport, or vehicle reload places the car outside the recorded bounds.
- Repaired Unicode dashboard symbols and preserved strict UTF-8 source handling.
- Preserved licenses, Auto Co-Driver settings and note edits, TrackLab courses and laps, RaceLink data, progression, achievements, sessions, crashes, and permanent Android signing compatibility.

## 2.2.1

- Auto Co-Driver setup and voice preview now appear before a TrackLab course has been created.
- Free Edition can use live Auto Co-Driver during TrackLab's existing 60-second per-session trial; Full Edition remains unlimited.

- Added better long-corner and tightening-corner calls with stronger route-noise suppression.
- Improved braking-zone detection and merges nearby braking instructions into the following corner call.
- Call timing now adapts to the speed recorded on the course while preserving Early, Normal, and Late modes.
- Preserved licenses, TrackLab courses and laps, Auto Co-Driver settings and note edits, RaceLink data, progression, achievements, sessions, and permanent Android signing compatibility.
